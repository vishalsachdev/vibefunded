VibeFunded visualizes a new funding model for open source software: memecoins with built-in royalty mechanisms. Instead of relying on grants, donations, or venture capital, OSS creators or community members can launch tokens where trading volume directly funds ongoing development.

How It Works

  1. OSS creators or community members launch memecoins on Bags.fm for OSS projects
  2. Each trade generates royalties that go to support the project
  3. Trading volume directly translates to project funding
  4. Network effects drive sustainable, perpetual revenue

Why It Matters

  • Disrupts traditional funding - No grant applications or VC pitches
  • Community-driven - Funding scales with project popularity
  • Risk-free for creators - Royalties provide ongoing maintenance funding
  • Transparent - All earnings are visible on-chain

Featured Projects

The dashboard tracks: Gastown ($GAS) by Steve Yegge, Ralph Wiggum Technique ($RALPH) by Geoffrey Huntley, Get Shit Done ($GSD) by Lex Christopherson, and LEON AI ($LEON) by Gren Louis.

Data updates every 30 minutes from DexScreener, Bags.fm, and CoinGecko APIs. This dashboard serves as a case study examining disruptive funding models in open source software and AI development.
